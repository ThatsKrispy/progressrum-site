Silver Blend · #V

Progress Rum
Silver

Sipping Silver · Cocktail Ready

Progress Rum Silver Blend #V is an exquisite fusion of aged and unaged rums that creates a truly unique drinking experience. Dubbed our "Sipping Silver Rum," this distinctive blend begins with aged Dominican and Trinidadian rums carefully charcoal-filtered to remove the color imparted by oak during aging.

The blend is then emboldened by an un-aged, funky pot still Jamaican rum and an un-aged blend of various lighter Caribbean rums. Silver Blend #V offers tantalizing tropical fruit and citrus aromas, followed by more subtle, nuanced notes of the aged rums.

Exceptionally smooth and mellow, this rum delights the palate with a long, luxurious finish and minimal burn. Perfect for mixing cocktails, yet gentle enough to be enjoyed neat or over a single ice cube. Cheers to Progress!

Aged Blend · #III

Progress Rum
Aged

Sipping Rum · Complex & Rich

Progress Rum's Aged Blend #III is an exquisite combination of four unique rum styles, each bringing its own special character to the spirit. The blend begins with a delicate and herbal 5-year-old Dominican Rum, distilled from fermented sugarcane juice.

This is followed by 5-year-old Venezuelan and Bajan Rums — both distilled from fermented molasses. The Venezuelan rum offers light floral notes while the stronger Bajan rum adds a touch of dry spice. The final addition is a robust 3-year-old Jamaican Rum, distilled in the traditional Jamaican style, adding intense depth and richness.

On the nose, Aged Blend #III opens with a subtle bouquet of floral notes, gradually revealing bolder fruit notes and hints of oak. On the palate, it evolves into a complex tapestry of dark chocolate, dried fruits, and spice — culminating in a smooth, lingering finish.
