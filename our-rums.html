PROGRESS RUM SILVER
PROGRESS RUM
SILVER

Progress Rum, Silver Blend # V, is an exquisite fusion of aged and unaged rums that creates a truly unique drinking experience. Dubbed our 'Sipping Silver Rum' – this distinctive blend begins with aged Dominican and Trinidadian rums that have been carefully charcoal-filtered to remove the color imparted by the oak during the aging process.

The blend is then emboldened by an un-aged, funky pot still Jamaican rum and an un-aged blend of various lighter Caribbean rums. Silver Blend # V offers a tantalizing array of vibrant tropical fruit and citrus aromas, followed by more subtle, nuanced notes of the aged rums.

Exceptionally smooth and mellow, this rum delights the palate with a long, luxurious finish and minimal burn. Perfect for mixing cocktails, yet gentle enough to be enjoyed neat or over a single ice cube, Progress Rum, Silver Blend # V is an experience you won't forget; CHEERS TO PROGRESS!

PROGRESS RUM AGED
PROGRESS RUM
AGED

Progress Rum's Aged Blend # III is an exquisite combination of four unique rum styles, each bringing its own special character to the spirit. The blend begins with a delicate and herbal 5-year-old Dominican Rum, distilled from fermented sugarcane juice. This is followed by 5-year-old Venezuelan and Bajan Rum, both distilled from fermented molasses.

The Venezuelan rum offers light floral notes while the stronger Bajan rum adds a touch of dry spice. The final addition is a robust 3-year-old Jamaican Rum, distilled in the traditional Jamaican style, which adds an intense depth and richness.

On the nose, Aged Blend # III opens with a subtle bouquet of floral notes, gradually revealing bolder fruit notes and hints of oak- derived from its careful aging. On the palate, it evolves into a complex tapestry of flavors, such as dark chocolate, dried fruits, and spice, which culminate in a smooth, lingering finish.
