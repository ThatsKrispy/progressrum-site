Heritage in a Bottle

Sip, Savor, and Celebrate

Looking for new ways to savor the exquisite flavors of Progress Rum? Whether you're a cocktail aficionado or new to the world of mixology, our rums offer a versatile foundation for an array of tantalizing drinks.

Below are some of our favorite concoctions that perfectly showcase the heritage and richness of our blends.

El Caribeño
  • 2 oz Progress Rum (Silver)
  • 4 oz Coconut Water
  • ¼ oz Lime Juice
  • ¼ oz Simple Syrup

A true Caribbean creation crafted with Progress Rum Silver.

Rum Sour
  • 2 oz Progress Aged Rum
  • ¼ oz Lime Juice
  • ¼ oz Lemon Juice
  • ¼ oz Honey
  • ¼ oz Egg White*

*Vegan substitute: Aquafaba

Daiquiri
  • 1 oz Silver
  • 1 oz Aged
  • ¾ oz Pineapple Juice
  • ½ oz Lime Juice
  • ½ oz Simple Syrup

*Mixed by Ryan of Big Cypress Distillery
